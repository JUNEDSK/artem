TELEMEDICINE DEVICES

Technical Features:

Digitizer
  • Advanced Scanning Technology:
    The Digitizer utilizes a high-resolution 5MP HD CMOS image sensor for crisp and detailed scans of health records, accommodating various document sizes.
  • Enhanced Illumination Control:
    The device features an LED light source with dimmer control, ensuring optimal visibility and image quality during radiograph scanning.
  • Seamless Connectivity:
    The Digitizer offers a USB 2.0 communication interface for easy and reliable connection with PCs or any other computing devices.
TELEMEDICINE DEVICES

Special Features:

Digitizer
  • Streamlined Health Record Digitization:
    The Digitizer's user-friendly interface simplifies the process of digitizing health records, including radiographs, reducing manual effort and improving workflow.
  • Versatile Document Scanning:
    In addition to radiographs, the Digitizer handles a wide range of standard documents, enabling comprehensive digitization for improved accessibility and collaboration.
  • Real-time Integration with Software Platform:
    The Digitizer seamlessly integrates with Artem's cloud-based software platform, ensuring instant uploading and viewing of digitized health records for streamlined medical workflows.

  • EMR Integration:
    The software platform supports Electronic Medical Record (EMR) capabilities, linking digitized records to patients' electronic profiles and promoting comprehensive patient information management.
  • Tele-Diagnostics and Telemedicine:
    By digitizing radiographs into DICOM format, the software platform enables remote access and reporting by radiologists, facilitating expert consultations and improved access to specialized care.
