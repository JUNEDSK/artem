    Seamlessly integrate with third-party CR/DR systems for efficient workflow.
  • 24x7 Online Reporting:
    Enjoy round-the-clock online reporting for X-Ray, CT, MRI, and Mammography.

Tele-Medicine Services

How It Works:

Tele-Radiology
  •  
    Masterful Configuration: Effortlessly integrate PACS Application Entity with CR/DR/ Console systems, ensuring a smooth and efficient setup process.
  •  
    Streamlined Image Upload: Install our intuitive image upload interface hassle-free, enabling swift and secure transfer of critical medical images.
  •  
    Unveiling the Invisible: Perform precise scans on patients, capturing invaluable data beneath the surface with expertise.
  •  
    Instant Image Upload: Seamlessly upload scanned images through our user-friendly interface, ensuring quick accessibility for reporting doctors.
  •  
    Automated Patient Details: Effortlessly capture patient information from scans, reducing manual entry. Technicians can easily update details using our intuitive registration interface.
  •  
    Enhanced Collaborative Diagnosis: Make uploaded images readily available to our esteemed panel of reporting doctors who leverage advanced diagnostic tools to unravel hidden complexities and provide accurate reports.
  •  
    Empowering Expert Analysis: Our doctors utilize cutting-edge diagnostic tools to gain deep insights from the images, assisting in delivering precise diagnoses and enhancing patient care.
  •  
    Effortless Report Accessibility: Download comprehensive reports in .docx or PDF format, facilitating seamless printing and sharing for easier decision-making.
  •  
    Support in Abnormal Cases: Seek a second opinion or engage in telephonic consultations with the reporting doctor in the event of an abnormal report. Our dedicated support ensures thorough analysis and peace of mind.

Tele-Radiology

Key Benefits

  • Improved access to radiology expertise:
    Ensure timely and accurate radiology interpretations, even in remote or underserved areas, benefiting patients with better diagnosis and treatment planning.
  • Faster turnaround time:
    Reduce waiting time for radiology reports by electronically transmitting and remotely interpreting images, enabling quicker diagnoses and treatment decisions, particularly in emergencies.
  • Extended coverage and 24/7 availability:
    Connect healthcare facilities with radiologists in different time zones, providing round-the-clock coverage and availability of radiology services, improving patient care and reducing delays in diagnosis.
  • Cost-effectiveness:
    Optimize resources and reduce costs by utilizing tele-radiology services on an as-needed basis, eliminating the need for full-time, on-site radiologists and minimizing patient travel expenses.
  • Specialist consultations and second opinions:
    Foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among radiology specialists through remote consultations, leading to improved diagnostic accuracy and confidence in complex cases.
  • Reduced medical errors:
    Enhance patient care and outcomes by facilitating timely and accurate interpretation of radiology images, minimizing errors associated with misinterpretation or delays in diagnosis.
  • Efficient workflow and productivity:
    Streamline radiology departments' workflow and increase productivity by integrating tele-radiology systems with PACS and EHR, enabling seamless transmission and storage of imaging data.
  • Continuity of Care:
    Promote continuity of care by facilitating easy access and sharing of patient information and imaging studies among healthcare providers, benefiting patients requiring ongoing monitoring or with complex medical histories.
