Tele-Medicine Services

How It Works:

Tele-Pathology
  •  
    Integration: Seamlessly connect all pathology equipment with data communication capabilities to our advanced platform, harnessing the power of interconnectedness.
  •  
    Configured for Success: Effortlessly customize tests, packages, and pricing within our system, providing a tailored approach that meets your specific needs.
  •  
    Streamlined Patient Registration: Enhance efficiency by swiftly registering patients, eliminating paperwork, and ensuring a seamless journey from start to finish.
  •  
    Barcoded Sample Collection: Collect samples and assign unique barcodes for accurate and traceable identification throughout the entire process.
  •  
    Unparalleled Testing: Utilize state-of-the-art equipment to conduct tests on samples, delivering precise results across a range of diagnostic procedures.
  •  
    Automatic Data Linkage: Leverage equipment integration to automatically link test data with the patient, reducing manual input and seamlessly connecting critical information.
  •  
    Effortless Submission: Streamline the process by enabling technicians to submit records for reporting once the data is complete, expediting results.
  •  
    Empowering Doctors: Our platform enables doctors to view test data and images, including integration with digital microscopes, facilitating comprehensive analysis and accurate diagnoses.
  •  
    Rapid Report Access: Instantly download, print, and share reports with patients through any medium once the diagnosis is approved by the doctor, prioritizing timely communication.
  •  
    Second Opinions and Re-tests: Facilitate second opinions and re-running of tests in case of abnormalities, ensuring comprehensive analysis and patient satisfaction.
Tele-Pathology

Key Features

  •  
    Rapid Turnaround Time: Instant report generation upon uploading test data, ensuring efficient results without compromising accuracy.
  •  
    Verified Reports: Each report is signed by the reporting doctor and includes their registration number, ensuring trust and authenticity.
  •  
    Telephonic Consultation: Arrange telephonic consultations with reporting doctors for personalized guidance and care when needed.
  •  
    Digitizing Laboratory Operations: Seamlessly digitize your entire laboratory workflow with our LIMS platform, enhancing efficiency and accuracy.
  •  
    Enhanced Patient Identification: Utilize unique patient identification for automated linkage of test reports with Electronic Health Records (EHR), simplifying data management.
  •  
    Expert Reporting Panel: Rely on our highly qualified team of MD Pathology specialists for accurate diagnoses and expert opinions.
  •  
    Comprehensive Reports: Receive A4-sized PDF reports with customizable letterheads, facilitating easy printing, sharing, and collaboration.
  •  
    Seamless Integration: Easily integrate our platform with third-party billing and CRM systems, optimizing workflow and efficiency.
  •  
    Flexible Payment Solutions: Manage transactions conveniently with our online wallet system, offering a pay-as-you-go facility.
  •  
    Mobile Applications: Empower your medical team with on-the-go access and enhanced productivity through our Doctor, Technician, and Phlebotomist mobile applications.
Tele-Pathology

Key Benefits

  • Remote Consultations:
    Pathologists can provide consultations remotely, collaborating and seeking expert opinions regardless of their physical location.
  • Faster Diagnoses:
    Real-time sharing and analysis of digital images reduce the time required for diagnosis and treatment decision-making.
  • Enhanced Accuracy and Quality:
    High-resolution image capture and automated capture of test data reduce human errors and improve diagnostic accuracy.
  • Cost and Time Efficiency:
    Remote access to cases and streamlined specimen digitization save time and resources, optimizing workflow.
  • Education and Training:
    Telepathology with equipment integration offers opportunities for remote education, training, and collaboration among pathologists.
  • Archiving and Data Management:
    Efficient archiving and integration with electronic health records (EHRs) support easy data retrieval and research.
  • Patient Care and Outreach:
    Extend services to underserved areas, remotely diagnose and monitor patients, and improve access to quality care.
