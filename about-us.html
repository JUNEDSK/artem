Driving Excellence
in Healthcare:
Redefining Care

We are a medical IT company based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, with a presence in 1500+ affiliations across India, as well as internationally in the Middle East and Africa. Our team has over 30 years of experience in providing healthcare and medical IT services.

Our goal is to make healthcare available, accessible, and affordable for all by promoting effective implementation using advanced technology solutions. We have developed a range of products and services to cater to healthcare stakeholders in both the private and public sectors.

MISSION

Shaping a Compassionate Tech-Infused Future

To enhance healthcare outcomes and drive cost-efficiency, Artem HealthTech leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our commitment is to make healthcare accessible and affordable for everyone, bridging gaps between rural and urban communities through advanced technology.

VISION

Pioneering Future -Ready Healthcare

Artem HealthTech envisions a future where innovative technology and compassionate care converge. Through global expansion and a strong presence in both the public and private sectors, we aim to create a world where healthcare is redefined, enabling better and more efficient care for all.
