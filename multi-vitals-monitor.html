TELEMEDICINE DEVICES

Technical Features:

Multi-Vitals Device
  • Integrated Medical Technologies:
    The Multi Vitals device combines essential medical technologies into a compact, modular design. It integrates ECG, SpO2, NIBP, Temperature, Digital Stethoscope, and ENT/Dermo/Dental Camera modules, providing a comprehensive solution for vital sign monitoring.
  • Effortless Connectivity:
    With a single USB output port, the device easily connects to any PC, simplifying data retrieval for further analysis and medical decision-making.
  • Comprehensive Data Capture:
    The Multi Vitals device provides digitized waveforms and numeric values, empowering healthcare professionals with detailed information for accurate diagnoses and personalized treatment plans.
TELEMEDICINE DEVICES

Special Features:

Multi-Vitals Device
  • Unmatched Portability:
    The device is compact and lightweight, allowing healthcare practitioners to carry it effortlessly, making vital sign monitoring accessible in various clinical settings or during telemedicine consultations.
  • Enhanced Integration:
    HL7 compliance enables seamless integration with third-party devices like blood analyzers and body composition analyzers, expanding diagnostic capabilities and improving patient care.
  • Empowering Software Platform:
    The cloud-based software platform transforms device-generated data into meaningful insights, facilitating efficient consultations, analysis, and reporting.
