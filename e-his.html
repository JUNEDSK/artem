EHIMS

Key Features
  • Patient Registration and Management:
    Easily handle patient demographics, contact details, medical history, and clinical information.
  • Integrated Information Systems:
    Seamlessly incorporate laboratory information systems (LIS),radiology information systems (RIS), and picture archiving and communication systems (ACS).
  • Electronic Health Record (EHR) Management:
    Efficiently store and manage electronic health records, including diagnostic reports, lab results, radiology reports, prescriptions, and treatment history.
  • Appointment Scheduling and Billing:
    Streamline appointment scheduling, billing, and payment processing, including insurance claims.
  • Inventory Tracking:
    Track and manage inventory of medical equipment, supplies, consumables, and pharmaceuticals.
  • Electronic Prescription:
    Enable physicians to prescribe medications electronically, ensuring accuracy and reducing errors.
  • Real-time Reporting and Analytics:
    Monitor clinical and operational performance with real-time reporting and analytics, tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) for continuous improvement.
  • Secure and Compliant Platform:
    Ensure data security with role-based access controls, data encryption, and compliance with healthcare data privacy regulations.
  • Telemedicine Capabilities:
    Enhance outreach and patient care with built-in telemedicine solutions.
  • Queue Management:
    Optimize patient flow and minimize waiting times by effectively managing patient queues

LIMS

Key Features
  • Sample Lifecycle Management:
    Efficiently handle sample tracking, test result tracking, and sample location management throughout the entire lifecycle.
  • Appointment Management:
    Manage appointments, including home visits and emergencies.
  • Test Management:
    Effectively manage laboratory tests, from test orders to specimen processing, test result entry, and quality control management.
  • Historical Data Comparison:
    Compare current results with the patient's historical data to identify trends and patterns.
  • Workflow Streamlining:
    Streamline laboratory workflows, including sample routing, priority settings, and test result routing.
  • Reporting and Analytics:
    Generate reports and analytics on laboratory performance, including test volumes, turnaround time, and error rates.
  • EHR Integration:
    Integrate pathology reports into electronic health records for comprehensive patient data management.
  • Inventory Management:
    Effectively manage laboratory inventory, including equipment, reagents, consumables, supplies, and asset maintenance tracking.
  • Regulatory Compliance:
    Ensure compliance with quality standards, patient data privacy laws, and health and safety regulations.
  • Electronic Signatures:
    Support electronic signatures for laboratory test results and reports to enhance data security and traceability.
  • Interoperability:
    Enable interoperability with other healthcare systems, such as electronic health records, laboratory information exchange, and clinical decision support systems.

CMS

Key Features
  • Patient Registration:
    Capture and manage essential patient information, such as name, contact details, and medical history.
  • Appointment Scheduling:
    Efficiently schedule and organize appointments with doctors, nurses, and healthcare providers.
  • Queue Management:
    Optimize patient flow and minimize waiting times by effectively managing patient queues.
  • Patient Consultation:
    Record and manage comprehensive patient consultations, including diagnosis, treatment plans, and medication prescriptions.
  • Billing and Claims Management:
    Streamline financial transactions for outpatient visits, including billing, invoicing, payment processing, and claims management.
  • Reporting and Analytics:
    Generate insightful reports and analytics on patient visits, appointment history, billing and claims, and other relevant metrics.
  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) Integration:
    Integrate OPD records and data into electronic health records for seamless patient data management.
  • Communication and Collaboration:
    Facilitate seamless communication and collaboration among healthcare providers, including appointment reminders, patient alerts, referrals and second opinions.
  • Security and Compliance:
    Ensure the security and compliance of patient data and healthcare operations in accordance with industry standards and regulations.
