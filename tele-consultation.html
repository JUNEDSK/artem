Tele-Consultation

What We Offer:

  • Teleconsultation Platform:
    Our tailored telemedicine solution provides a user-friendly platform for seamless consultations with general practitioners.
  • Electronic Health Record (EHR) Management System:
    Efficiently manage patient records with our advanced EHR system.
  • Online Specialist Consultation:
    Schedule appointments with our expert panel of specialists for convenient virtual consultations.
  • IoMT-Enabled Telemedicine Devices:
    Enhance online consultations with cutting -edge devices like Vital Prime, 12 Lead ECG, Digitizer, and Pan Camera.
Tele-Medicine Services

How It Works:

Tele-Consultation
  •  
    Patient check-in at the clinic, receptionist registers details.
  •  
    Doctor reviews medical history, conducts physical examination, and documents findings on the platform.
  •  
    Multi Vitals device captures essential signs and automatically syncs with the system.
  •  
    Digitizer feature links paper-based health records to EHR.
  •  
    Treating doctor schedules specialist appointments through the platform.
  •  
    Specialist accesses patient history and vital data from patient timelines.
  •  
    Virtual consultation with a specialist, diagnosis, and treatment plan provided.
  •  
    Treating doctor downloads concise consultation summary.
  •  
    Summary includes diagnosis, treatment plan, prescriptions, and tests.
  •  
    Patient takes the prescription/ treatment plan to the pharmacy or diagnostic center for further action.
